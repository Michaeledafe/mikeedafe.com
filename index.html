Mike Edafe

Early Life

Evezi Mike Edafe, born on May 19th 1965 in Okpe-isoko Delta State Nigeria. Brought up in a family of 12, where his father married two wives in which his mother had four children (third born from his mother), and his step mother eight chidlen respectivly.

Family


In 1997 he got married to Ann Oruamabo, and has five children. A girl, Annette Edafe and four boys, Michael Edafe, Marvellous Edafe, Treasure Edafe and David Edafe. He currently lives with his family in Eneka, Port-Harcourt Rivers state.

Education


Primary School: Orie primary school isoko-south Delta state (1971-1976)
Secondary school: Emore grammer school Ole (1976-1981)
Tertiary institution: Amborse Ali University Ekpoma, Edo State (1982-1986)

Self Development

  • Diploma in Computer Science from the University of Port Harcourt.
  • Higher diploma in Computer Science from Fedral University of Technology Owerri
  • Personal Certification in Computer Science

Work experience

In 1990, still in Delta state, he got a job in First Bank of Nigeria. He started from the main branch in Port-Harcourt, then was later transferred to Bonny island where he worked for eleven years, he was then transferred to other branches such as Abonima, Onne and Diobu which he works currently as a senior staff and the head of the branch, all still in Rivers state. During his years at the bank, he was appointed to different levels and Positions.

Head Positions

  • Secetary of the national union of banks insurance and financial employee(NUBIFE).
  • Vice chairman (NUBIFE).
  • Regional chairman of the association of senior staff of banks insurance and financial employees(ASSBIFE).
  • Chairman of the south-south region of first bank unit.
  • Special advisor on economic and emopowerment to the chairman of the trade union congress(TUC) in Rivers state.
